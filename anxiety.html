Anxiety

As the scarlet blood pumps through your heart

gradually increasing speed as the seconds hasten,

eyes search frantically, while skin chafes aganist skin.

A clock ticks with a metallic scratch,

like a shreiks of a dying bird.

Bones rattle red.

Bugs cylinder down the throat

as lightning strikes the pulse.

A needle pierces the yellow fish eyes

draining all the hope.
