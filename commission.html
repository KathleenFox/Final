Commission

If you are interested in having my design something to put on Redbubble, whether it be a quote or a small sketch, I will do it for a small fee. Considering that Redbubble already charges a pretty hefty fee for any of it's product it won't be too much, but I do need to charge something because the artist comission from Redbubble is usually around 10%. And believe me, it's not a great comission when people are only buying $2 per sticker. So contact me if you want a design done for Redbubble. For a quote it will be $5 and $8 for a sketch. Let me know if you're interested.
