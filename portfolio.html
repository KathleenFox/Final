Artist Statement

I am Kathleen Fox, a freshmen Interactive Multimedia major and Creative Writing minor at The College of New Jersey, with intentions of obtaining a career in animation. My goal is to help bring stories to life and tell them in new, intriguing ways through different mediums. A lot of my work has darker elements in it, like death, the true end to everyone's story. My work here is done in Final Cut X, Audacity and Photoshop.
